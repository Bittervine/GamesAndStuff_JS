Thorium Gap Manual

A short reference for weapons, pickups, difficulty, combo, and respawn rules.

Back to game Project notes

Goal

Survive each stage, score as much as possible, and defeat the boss at the end of every level.

Core Flow

  • Destroy enemies to score points and make pickups drop.
  • Collect weapon wrenches to upgrade or switch weapons.
  • Collect shields, bombs, rapid-fire, magnet, and score pickups as they appear.
  • Each level ends with a boss battle.

Controls

  • B or SPACE launches a bomb.

Shield And Health

  • Shields are shown as a faint bubble around the ship.
  • Each shield point blocks one hit and is then consumed.
  • If you have no shields, hits damage the ship directly.
  • Ship damage is repaired slowly over time if you avoid taking new hits.
  • Ship body damage is shown visually as sparks on the hull.

Weapons

  • The game has five weapon families. Each weapon has five tiers: I, II, III, IIII, and V.
  • Picking up a wrench for current weapon upgrades that to next tier. At V, another weapon may be updated instead (up to III).
  • Picking up a wrench for a different weapon switches to that wepon.
  • When a ship is lost, the current weapon loses one tier. The new ship use your strongest weapon.

Weapon Upgrades

Difficulty

  • Easy: lighter enemy health, slower enemies, lower spawn pressure, and full player damage.
  • Normal: Player does two thirds the damage as on Easy. A little more enemies too.
  • Hard: tougher, faster, and more crowded enemy waves, while player shots do one third of the Easy damage.

Combo And Overdrive

  • The combo counter increases every time you destroy an enemy.
  • The combo timer window is short, so you must keep destroying enemies quickly to keep the chain alive.
  • If you stop killing enemies for too long, the combo resets to 0.
  • At combo 3 and above, the game briefly shows a COMBO +N banner after each kill.
  • At combo 10, Overdrive triggers.
  • When Overdrive triggers, the combo counter is cleared and the bonus state begins.
  • Overdrive is a temporary power state that makes the game feel more intense and rewarding for a strong kill streak.

Other Pickups

  • Rapid Fire: temporarily lowers shot delay.
  • Shield: adds a shield point, up to three. Each protects from a single hit and is then consumed.
  • Bomb: adds a bomb, up to four.
  • Magnet: pulls pickups toward the player for a short time.
  • Score: bonus points.
