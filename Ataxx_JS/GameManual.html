Ataxx Manual

Goal: finish with more stones than the AI.

  • A legal move starts from one of your stones and lands on an empty square one step away (clone) or two steps away (jump).
  • Cloning keeps the source stone. Jumping empties the source square.
  • After landing, any adjacent enemy stones flip to your color.
  • If a player has no legal move, the turn passes.
  • The game ends when the board is full or nobody can move.
