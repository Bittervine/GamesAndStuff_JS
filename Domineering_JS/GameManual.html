Domineering Manual

Goal: leave the AI with no legal domino placement.

  • Each turn adds one new domino to the board; already placed dominoes never move again.
  • A legal move is placing a domino on two empty orthogonally adjacent squares in your required orientation.
  • You place vertical dominoes.
  • The AI places horizontal dominoes.
  • The yellow outline marks the most recently placed domino.
  • If a player cannot move, that player loses.
